## RSA - generate Public/Private Key Pair
Calculate the formulas:
. n = p * q 
. φ(n) = (p - 1) * (q - 1) 
. 1 < e < φ(n) 
. (d * e) % φ(n) = 1
. Kpub = (e, n) 
. Kpri = (d, n)

Encrypt/Decrypt:  
. c = m^e mod n
. m = c^d mod n
